Find out what you need to travel to Canada

You need a visa to travel or transit through Canada.

You can apply for a:

  • Tourist visa
  • Transit visa
    • Transit without a visa if you are flying to/from the U.S from Beijing, Shanghai...
  • Parents or grandparents visa (stay in Canada up to two years at a time)
  • Business visa

You said you are:

  • traveling with a passport from China
  • not a permanent resident of the U.S.
